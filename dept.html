Department of Wildlife Conservation

The Wildlife Conservation Department of Sri Lanka is a dedicated government agency focused on preserving the nation's rich biodiversity. Through proactive conservation efforts, habitat management, and community engagement, the department safeguards diverse ecosystems and ensures the sustainable coexistence of wildlife and human populations.
